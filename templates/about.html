Severity Prediction Explore & Visualize About Dataset Team Introduction

Project Introduction

Our goal is to develop a platform for car accident severity prediction that can predict the category of severity with different impact on traffic delay in US as well as characterize the various factors affecting accident severity. Meanwhile the platform will include a series of data visualization functionalities that can help the users understand the large historical accident dataset in an effective and interactive way.
